DiscoverEU Memories Journal

DiscoverEU Memories Journal is a DiscoverEU Inclusion project that brought together young people to explore Europe through train travel, creativity, reflection, and intercultural learning.

The project created space for young people, including participants with fewer opportunities, to travel across different European cities, discover new cultures, meet local communities, and express their experiences through journals, photos, stories, and creative reflections.

This website presents the collective memories of all 18 people involved in the project, including accompanying persons. Each group followed a different route and documented their journey in their own creative way.

About Erasmus+ and DiscoverEU

Erasmus+

Erasmus+ is the European Union programme supporting education, training, youth, and sport. It creates opportunities for young people to learn, travel, participate in international activities, and develop important personal, social, and professional skills.

DiscoverEU

DiscoverEU gives young people the opportunity to explore Europe mainly by train. It helps them experience cultural diversity, understand European values, become more independent, and feel more connected to Europe.

Through the DiscoverEU Inclusion Action, young people with fewer opportunities receive additional support so they can participate on equal terms with others.

Project Aim

The aim of DiscoverEU Memories Journal is to combine travel, inclusion, creativity, and European identity.

Through this project, participants were encouraged to:

  • Explore different European cities by train
  • Develop confidence in travelling
  • Understand different cultures and lifestyles
  • Reflect on inclusion, diversity, and European values
  • Express their experiences through creative journals
  • Share their memories with others through digital dissemination

The project is not only about visiting places. It is about learning from the journey, building self-confidence, sharing personal perspectives, and showing how travel can support inclusion and intercultural understanding.

Our Collective Journal

The main result of the project is the DiscoverEU Memories Journal, a collective booklet created from the reflections, photos, journal entries, and creative contributions of the participants.

The booklet brings together different voices, routes, cities, and experiences into one shared project memory.

Dissemination and Impact

The project results are shared to make the experience visible beyond the group itself. Through the website, booklet, group journals, photos, and videos, we aim to inspire other young people to discover Erasmus+, DiscoverEU, and international youth opportunities.

The project shows how travel can support inclusion, creativity, self-expression, and a stronger sense of European belonging.
